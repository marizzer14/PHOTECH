About Us

PhoTech is more than just a photo printing service; we're a student-run business corporation dedicated to blending creativity with innovation. As part of our Entrepreneurship and Empowerment Technology project at Don Bosco Senior High School Paranaque, we aim to provide affordable, convenient, and high-quality photo printing services to the school community. We turn digital photos into lasting physical prints using custom-designed templates and premium materials.

Our Vision

We envision a school community where photo printing is convenient, accessible, and affordable for every student—without the need to travel far or wait in long lines.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver fast, affordable, and high-quality photo printing services tailored specifically for students. We aim to reduce the stress of errands by bringing printing closer to home or campus, using available resources and student-friendly prices.

Service Page

Capture Your Memories!

Ready to create lasting memories? Here's how our service works:

Customize My Own Photo

  1. Upload Your Photo or Schedule a Photo Op: You can either upload your favorite photo directly from your device, or schedule a convenient time for a professional photo session with our team.
  2. Enter Your Information: Please provide your name, grade and section, and contact number in the fields below. This information helps us keep track of your order and deliver your photos efficiently.
  3. Instant Order ID: Once you submit your information and photo (or schedule your session), you'll receive a unique Order ID immediately. Keep this ID handy for any inquiries.
  4. Secure and Private: Rest assured that your photos are handled with the utmost privacy and care. Once you upload your photo, it's directly and securely sent to our email. Only authorized personnel have access to it, ensuring that your personal moments remain private.
  5. Order Tracking (Name - OrderID): You can view a list of names and corresponding Order IDs on the side panel. This allows you to confirm that your order has been received and is being processed.

Customize Your Template

Design your own template, add stickers, text, and upload images. We'll send your design to our team!
