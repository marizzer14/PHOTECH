About Us

Fun-e-Learning Corporation is a locally SEC registered corporation created to bring out the best from Filipino learners.
Taking advantage of the expertise and experience of the people of Fun-e-Learning, we created educational materials for the Filipino learners.

MISSION / VISION

The mission of Fun-e-Learning is to provide the Filipino learners with video materials useful to their growth and applicable to their daily lives.

The vision of Fun-e-Learning is to have a significant part in making the Filipinos among the top learners in the world.
